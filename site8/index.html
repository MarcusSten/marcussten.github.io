Features

Manage work more effectively.

Oversee more than just an estimate.

Use the Cards feature to create estimates, assign tasks to workshop and office people in your team and track progress of each from Estimate to Invoice.

Give your techs Digital Job cards

Allow all techs in your workshop to view the items of work estimated for, use like a checklist for improved technician process and productivity management.

Create Task reminders for office and workshop team members

Create tasks for any team member or for yourself to enable efficient internal process management or for improved customer service.

Simplify the estimate creation process

Create estimates quickly with our easy to navigate item menus that apply your pre-set rates and times, quick view and image zoom functions as well as record assessor adjustments according to different

Create Estimate Templates

Create templates of the estimate rates and times of common repair types and quickly apply to new ones. Save time rewriting common estimates over and over again.

Apply Assessor adjustments

Easily apply insurance assessor adjustments to your estimate to match your totals with theirs.

Allow adjustments to auto update from insurer claims management systems.

Integrated with Estimage, ORM and more to come.

Manage invoices with ease

Easily create and track excess and complete invoices, mark them as awaiting payment or Paid to reconcile payments.

Email and print copies for your customers all from repair-shop.

Customise and present professional invoices for your customers

Add you logo and your business details to create professional quality invoices.

Synchronise your invoices with accounting software

Send & update your invoices to popular accounting software like XERO to reduce manual data entry and invoice reconciliation. (MYOB, Quicken SAGE integration to come)
